# How Will Humans Use AI?
### A 2-Book Series from a Non-Technical CEO's Real Experience

I'm a CEO of a small company in Vietnam. No technical background.

In March 2026, I built a complete AI system for my business. 
Then my laptop got stolen. I rebuilt everything in 48 hours.

During that process, I discovered 3 things AI will never tell you first.

## 3 Things AI Will Never Tell You First

**1. Context Drift** — In long conversations, AI may work from 
an assumption replaced 30 messages ago. Neither of you noticed.

**2. Memory Illusion** — The feeling that AI "knows" you is real. 
That's exactly the problem. It's a new instance every session.

**3. Emotional Calibration** — AI mirrors your emotional state. 
Dangerous when you need objective business analysis.
